Protein Pancakes

Thin, wide protein-pancakes blending the fluffy essence of buttermilk classics with crepe-like elegance. Crafted from egg whites, whole eggs, rolled oats, lacotse-free milk, and a drizzle of raw organic honey. The sweet honey accents their wholesome, guilt-free appeal while acting as a natural and healthy sweetener. High protein, low calories, naturally sweetened, easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 90g Egg Beaters
  • 1x Raw Egg
  • 40g Quaker Oats, 100% Whole Grain Rolled Oats, Old Fashioned
  • 0.5 cup Lactaid Fat Free Milk
  • 14g Raw Organic Honey

Nutrition Facts (per serving)

  • Calories: 384
  • Fat: 8g
  • Carbs: 44g
    • Fiber: 6.4g
    • Sugars: 11.3g
  • Protein: 28g

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients.
  2. Add Egg Beaters, raw egg, oats, and milk to blender.
  3. Blend together to create a batter.
  4. Heat pan to medium.
  5. Apply a small spray of low calorie cooking oil to prevent sticking to pan.
  6. Pour batter to form a thin and wide pancake.
  7. Cook 2-3 minutes per side until golden.
  8. Stack pancakes.
  9. Drizzle with honey.
