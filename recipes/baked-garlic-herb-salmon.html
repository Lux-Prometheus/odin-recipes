Baked Garlic and Herb Salmon

Tender, flavorful baked salmon fillet seasoned with garlic, dill, and lemon. Lean, high-protein, low-calorie dinner for one. Made with a single salmon filet, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, dill, salt, and pepper. The fresh herbs and zesty lemon create a delicious healthy meal. High protein, low calories, easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 6 oz Salmon Fillet, skinless
  • 1.5 Teaspoons Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1.5 Teaspoons Lemon Juice
  • 1x Garlic Clove, minced
  • 1 Teaspoon Fresh Dill, finely chopped
  • 1/8th Teaspoon of Kosher Salt
  • 1x Pinch of Freshl Ground Black Pepper

Nutrition Facts (per serving)

  • Calories: 280
  • Fat: 15g
  • Carbs: 2g
    • Fiber: 0g
    • Sugars: 0g
  • Protein: 34g

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Whisk together oil, lemon juice, garlic, dill, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Place salmon in a baking dish and coat with marinade.
  4. Let marinate for 15 minutes at room temperature.
  5. PBake for 12-15 minutes until salmon flakes easily with a fork (internal temperature 145°F)
  6. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
